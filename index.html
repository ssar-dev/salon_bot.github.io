Introduction

Welcome to the demo version of SaloonBot.

Please note that the text interface may not be fully enabled. Some functionalities may also be incompatible with your computer's security settings.

How may I help you today?

• Book appointments and send a confirmation email.

• Answer queries on services and opening times.

• Make small talk. You're welcome to try typing random phrases, but I can only reply to common ones. Try "Good morning." or "Give me a hug."
